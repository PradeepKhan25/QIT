A Legacy Reimagined:

GACAKUM Munnai

The history of education in Tamil Nadu is incomplete without mentioning the Government Arts College (Autonomous), Kumbakonam. To preserve the bond between the institution and its scholars, the

Munnai Maanakkar Sangam

serves as the official platform for connection. Through

GACAKUM Munnai

, we invite every graduate to return to their roots and contribute to the ongoing success of our alma mater.

Our college, often called the "Cambridge of South India," has produced legendary thinkers. The

Arasu Kalai Kalloori Munnai Maanakkar Sangam

is dedicated to ensuring that the brilliance of our past informs the potential of our future. By visiting

gacakummunnai.in

, you can join a network that spans generations of excellence.

Building the Future via

GACAKUM Munnai

The primary vision of the

Government Arts College Kumbakonam alumni

GAC Munnai

gacakummunnai.in
